## When to use it

Use when sales needs an internal first draft for positioning against a competitor.

## Inputs needed

- Competitor name
- Product area
- Customer segment
- Known opportunity context

## Workflow steps

- Gather mocked CRM, workspace, and knowledge context.
- Summarize positioning, proof points, traps to avoid, and discovery questions.
- Identify unsupported claims that need validation.
- Keep the battlecard internal.

## Expected output format

- Competitor snapshot
- Telnyx strengths
- Objection handling
- Discovery questions
- Claims needing validation

## Safety notes

- Internal-only by default.
- Do not send unapproved competitive claims to customers.
